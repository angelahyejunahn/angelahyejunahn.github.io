PROCESS
Research
I always start with research - depending on what I'm trying to find out, I would:
  • Work with project managers to gather business requirements and background information
  • Conduct user interviews and contextual inquiries
  • Interview sales and support team members
  • Create user journey maps to identify gaps in existing design or process
  • Create data flows
  • Conduct competitive analysis
Define Problem
Surface-level problems are typically instances of deeper, underlying problems. I ask myself 'why' to identify the actual problem I need to solve.
Ideate
With potential constraints in mind, I come up with 2 to 3 different solutions. I present my ideas to the rest of the team, subject matter experts, and other team members. By sharing my ideas early on, I receive useful feedback and iterate on my designs.
